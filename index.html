Yevheniia Baranova

  • Why I chose design

    I chose design because it allows me to express my creativity and turn ideas into something visual and meaningful. I enjoy creating things that are both beautiful and functional.

  • What inspires me

    I am inspired by the world around me — nature, music, and the emotions people express. I love noticing small details, textures, and atmosphere, and turning them into visual ideas.

  • My goal

    My goal is to create designs that inspire people and make them feel something. I want my work to tell stories and have meaning.
